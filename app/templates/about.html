OUR STORY

Founded by passionate snowboarders and videographers, Momentum Clips started with a simple idea: everyone deserves to have their best moments on the slopes captured professionally.

We've spent years perfecting our craft, combining cutting-edge filming technology with expert editing powered by AI innovation to create videos that capture the thrill, beauty, and excitement of snowboarding.

Based in the stunning Bansko, Bulgaria, we're positioned at one of Europe's premier snowboarding destinations. Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned pro, we're here to tell your story on the mountain.
